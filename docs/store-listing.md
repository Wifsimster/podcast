# Play Store listing copy — Ondes

Ready-to-paste text for the Google Play Console store listing. Character limits:
**title ≤ 30**, **short description ≤ 80**, **full description ≤ 4000**.
Verify the live count in the Console (emoji can count as 2).

---

## English (en-US)

**App name**
```
Ondes — Podcast Player
```

**Short description** (≤80)
```
Ad-free, private podcast player: downloads, Android Auto, sleep timer & more
```

**Full description** (≤4000)
```
Ondes is a clean, fast, ad-free podcast player for Android — no ads, no
analytics, no account. Just your shows.

Discover podcasts with built-in search or paste any RSS feed, then listen the
way you like: full background playback with lock-screen and notification
controls, variable speed, skip silence, and a volume boost for clearer speech.

FEATURES
• Background playback with lock-screen & notification controls
• Android Auto — browse and play hands-free in the car
• Variable speed (0.8×–3×), with per-podcast speed overrides
• Skip silence & volume boost for clearer speech
• Configurable skip intervals and auto-play of the next episode
• Chapters and rich, tappable show notes with timestamps that seek
• Sleep timer — fixed durations or stop at end of episode
• Offline downloads, with optional Wi-Fi-only and auto-delete when finished
• Background refresh and new-episode notifications
• Resume where you left off, with a persistent Up-Next queue
• Discover podcasts or add any RSS feed yourself
• Subscriptions library with pull-to-refresh and per-show filtering
• Own your data: OPML import/export and full local backup/restore
• Material You dynamic theming, light/dark/system, edge-to-edge
• Localized in English and French
• Accessibility: full TalkBack support with custom actions

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No ads. No analytics. No login. Everything — subscriptions, progress,
downloads and settings — stays on your device.

Ondes is an independent player. It streams the publicly published RSS feeds you
subscribe to and is not affiliated with any podcast or publisher.
```

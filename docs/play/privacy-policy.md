# Privacy Policy — Carne

_Last updated: 2026-06-06_

> Replace "Carne" with the final app name before publishing. Host this page at a
> public URL and enter that URL in the Play Console.

Carne ("the app") is a podcast player. We designed it to respect your privacy:
**the app has no account, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking.**

## Data we collect
**None.** Carne does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or
to any third party we control. There are no user accounts and no sign-in.

All of your data — subscriptions, playback positions, downloads, and settings —
is stored **locally on your device**. You can export or delete it at any time
from within the app (OPML export and full backup/restore).

## Network connections
To do its job, the app connects directly to:
- **Podcast feed servers** you subscribe to (to fetch RSS feeds and episode audio).
- **Apple's iTunes Search API** (only when you search for or browse podcasts), which receives your search terms.

These requests are made over HTTPS where the server supports it. We do not add
identifiers to these requests and we do not receive any of this traffic.

## Permissions
- **Internet / network state** — to download feeds and stream/download episodes.
- **Foreground service & notifications** — for background playback and new-episode alerts.
- **Modify audio settings** — for the optional "Boost volume" effect.

## Children
The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
